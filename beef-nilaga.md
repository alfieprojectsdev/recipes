### **Beef Nilaga (Instant Pot)**
*Yield: 5 Servings | Prep time: 15 mins | Cook time: 45 mins*

---

## ingredients
* 1 kg Beef brisket or shank
* 3 Potatoes (quartered)
* 1 large Cabbage or 2 bunches Pechay
* 1 large Onion (quartered)
* 1.5 tbsp Peppercorns & Fish sauce (patis)

## instructions
1. Place beef, onion, peppercorns, and 6 cups water in the IP.
2. Set to **Manual High Pressure for 40 mins**.
3. Quick release. Add potatoes; set to Saute mode for 7 mins.
4. Add cabbage/pechay in the last 2 minutes. Season with patis to taste.

## additional notes / alternative ingredients
* **Alt:** Add 2 corn-on-the-cob (cut into thirds) for more volume.
