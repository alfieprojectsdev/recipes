### **Instant Pot Munggo**
*Yield: 5-6 Servings | Prep time: 5 mins | Cook time: 35 mins*

---

## ingredients
* 1.5 cups Dry mung beans (no soak)
* 400g Pork cubes or 2 pieces tinapa
* 2 chicken cubes
* Garlic, onion, ginger
* 2 cups Malunggay or Alugbati leaves

## instructions
1. Saute pork, garlic, onions, and ginger in the IP.
2. Add dry beans and 6-7 cups of water.
3. Close lid. Set to **Manual High Pressure for 30 mins**.
4. Natural release. Stir in the green leaves using residual heat.

## additional notes / alternative ingredients
* **Sub:** Add sliced bitter melon (ampalaya) if you want that traditional bitterness.
